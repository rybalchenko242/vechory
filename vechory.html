about us

Welcome to Vechory Venice, where we're a passionate group of Ukrainian students and volunteers in Venice, all rallying for a cause close to our hearts: supporting Ukraine.

our aim?

To create amazing events that bring a taste of modern Ukraine to Venice while raising essential funds for those in need back home. Join us in our journey of cultural celebration and charitable action. Together, we're making an impact, one event at a time, spreading hope and solidarity to Ukraine.
